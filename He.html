Helium Properties

Helium is a chemical element with the symbol He and atomic number 2. Here are some of its key properties:

Physical Properties

  • Atomic Number: 2
  • Atomic Mass: 4.0026 u
  • State at Room Temperature: Gas
  • Melting Point: -272.20°C
  • Boiling Point: -268.93°C
  • Density: 0.1786 kg/m³
